Team

ISE 195B: Senior Design Team

Summary

Used DMADV (Define, Measure, Analyze, Design, Verify) Six Sigma model for a manufacturing optimization project at a computer storage device company. One improvement initiative was to develop a streamlined User Interface based on worker feedback/requirements.

Other responsibilites: develop Mathematical Model (Operations Research) using LINGO to improve worker utilization, contribute to building ProModel simulation of the manufacturing process, define/map process using Swimlane Flowchart to assist in identifying areas of improvement, and contribute to other areas of project/report such as Design of Experiments (MiniTab), Financial Analysis (Excel), and recommending/supporting improvements.
