Summary

For my senior project (ISE 195B), I used the DMADV (Define, Measure, Analyze, Design, Verify) Six Sigma model for a manufacturing optimization project at a computer storage device company. One improvement initiative was to develop a streamlined User Interface based on worker feedback/requirements and Non-Value Addeds (NVAs) identified on the previous Interface.

Other responsibilites: develop Mathematical Model (Operations Research) using LINGO to improve worker utilization and manufacturing output, contribute to building ProModel simulation of the manufacturing process, define/map process using Swimlane Flowchart to assist in identifying areas of improvement (NVAs vs. VAs), and contribute to other areas of project/report such as Design of Experiments (MiniTab), Financial Analysis (Excel), and recommending/supporting improvements.
