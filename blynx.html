Team

Intel RPC: Blynx

Summary

Leader of Team Blynx who designed, developed, and presented a prototype navigational device for visually-impaired people based on research, feedback, and requirements. Team Blynx is one of the 20 initial teams to make it to the Final Milestone of the Intel Rapid Prototyping Challenge. Of the 9 qualifying teams at the Final Milestone located at Intel, Team Blynx was awarded 2nd place.
