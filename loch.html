Team

SJSU Robotics: Mission Control Design Engineers

Summary

Developed and bug-tested the Arm & End Effector component of the Mission Control Interface required for SJSU Robotics' rover (Loch) to compete in an international collegiate competition (University Rover Challenge 2017) hosted by The Mars Society at the Mars Desert Research Station. Also served as Treasurer and Lead of Business Team.
