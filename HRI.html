Team

SJSU Usability Systems Engineering Research (USER) Lab

Summary

Assisted in a graduate-level research project involving human participants and their interactions with a robot (named Yolanda). Used SolidWorks and MakerBot to develop and 3D print modifications to the robot. Also helped in the Design of Experiments (SPSS) after collecting data from over 30 samples.

Tools

Excel, MakerBot, SolidWorks, SPSS

Industrial/Systems Engineering

CAD, Design of Experiments, Statistical Analysis
