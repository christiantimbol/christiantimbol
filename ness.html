Team

SJSU Robotics: Mechanical Design Engineers

Summary

Member of Project Nessie's Mechanical Design Engineering Team responsible for the design and manufacturing of the Chassis & Suspension. Created CADs of sheet metal parts to be used for the Chassis as well as acrylic pegboards (that are integrated onto the Chassis). The acrylic pegboards were used to hold the electrical components. Project Nessie is SJSU's competitive rover for the 2016 University Rover Challenge, an international collegiate challenge where universities competed in Hanksville, Utah, to see who designed the most "Mars-ready" rover!
